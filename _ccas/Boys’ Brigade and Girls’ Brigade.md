<p>The Boys’ Brigade (BB) is the world’s first uniformed organisation for
youth and The Girls’ Brigade (GB) is one the of the oldest uniformed youth
organisation in Singapore. Our companies, BB 24J Singapore Company and
GB 14<sup>th</sup> Company, aim to provide opportunities for our Boys and
Girls to discover their interests and talents, and platforms to for them
to learn values, develop and grow in moral character, social-emotional
competencies, and other relevant skills to prepare them for future challenges.</p>
