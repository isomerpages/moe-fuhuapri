<p>Applied Learning Programme provides meaningful and purposeful age-appropriate
opportunities for real-life application of learning. They allow for experimentation,
self-discovery and joy of learning.</p>
<h4><strong>STUDENT AS DESIGNER FOR THE 21ST CENTURY</strong></h4>
<p>"Student as Designer for the 21st Century" is a 6-year programme that
centers on computational thinking, using coding applications and the design
thinking protocol to equip our students with the skills to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Decompose</strong> – Break down complex problems into smaller,
more manageable parts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Recognize Patterns</strong> – Identify similarities within and
across problems.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Abstract</strong> – Focus on key information while omitting irrelevant
details.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Develop Algorithms</strong> – Create step-by-step solutions and
establish rules to solve problems.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>By the end of Primary 6, our students will be able to:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Showcase 21st Century Competencies through Computational Thinking and
Critical Thinking Skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build problem-solving abilities for the future</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Acquire ICT skills essential for the future workforce</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Contribute to the community by leveraging technology</p>
</li>
</ul>
